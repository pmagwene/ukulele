LEFT HAND FINGER DEXTERITY EXERCISE 3 -- "Up the Neck and Stretch"
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n0iYXmDiZJE

* limber up fingers
* index finger: fret 1
* ring finger: fret 3
* pinky: fret 5
* shift up by one fret after each round, moving up the neck
* Going down, index on fret 5, fingers down
